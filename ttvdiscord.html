What is TTV about?

The Territorial TV is a news station that covers the Territorial.io game and community.
The TTV Discord server is a platform where the TTV and its audience can interact and exchange information related to the game and its community.
It is an unbiased and rapid news service, and it is the most popular news service.
We promote justice and independent journalism.
