History

2020

The development of the territorial.io probably began in the spring of 2020.

On April 12, a YouTube account was registered.

Since July 2020, it has been possible to view statistics about attacks.

On August 3, the game was uploaded to Google Play. One of the first sites with an apk version of Territrorial.io, not counting Google Play, was the apkgk.

In September, the activity of the game increased.

On November 1, the game was downloaded more than 1000 times on Google Play, and the game's activity increased significantly. At the end of December 2020, the game was downloaded more than 10,000 times on Google Play, and the activity increased more than 3 times.

On December 30, more than a million attacks were made.

2021

On January 23, more than 2 million attacks were made.

On April 10, 2021, there was a strong jump in activity, and the game was downloaded more than 100,000 times on Google Play.

June was the most active month for the Territorial.io. More than 300 players played on it every day at the same time. And on June 20, the mark reached 376 players at the same time.

In July, the number of downloads on Google Play exceeded 200,000.

2022

Moderators have appeared in the discord server, the server is updated frequently.

In February, discord server Territorial Coin was created. A discord server template was also created.

In March, the number of members in the discord server exceeded 13 000. A League of Clans was created.

In April, the community Territorial.io participated in r/place. The number of members in the discord server exceeded 15 000. Zombie mode has been added.

In May, a new discord server was created, the previous one was archived.
